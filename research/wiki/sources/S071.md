# Yu Wenhao: Karpathy Zettelkasten comparison

> Critical analysis | 2026-04

## 1. Core Claim
- Karpathy's LLM wiki approach aggregates knowledge by topic, which differs fundamentally from Zettelkasten's atomic concept methodology; while maintenance cost is near-zero with LLM assistance, the "container boundary" problem remains unresolved.

## 2. Method / Mechanism
- **Topic Aggregation (Karpathy)**: Groups related ideas into topic-level wiki articles.
- **Atomic Concepts (Zettelkasten / LYT)**: Each note contains one idea, linked through explicit references.
- **Container Boundary Problem**: Where does a topic article end? What belongs inside vs. outside a given container? Karpathy's approach lacks explicit rules for this.
- **Maintenance Cost**: Near-zero because the LLM handles compilation and updates; contrast with manual Zettelkasten curation.
- **Memex Vision**: Positions Karpathy's approach as a modern realization of Bush's 80-year Memex vision.

## 3. Key Evidence
- Comparative analysis of two well-established knowledge-management methodologies.
- Explicit identification of the "container boundary" as an open design question in Karpathy's system.

## 4. Limitations & Gaps
- **Source inaccessible**: HTML archive only contains a redirect to `/zh-TW/`; substantive content not captured.
- Content relies entirely on reading-log summary.
- No empirical user study comparing Zettelkasten vs. LLM-wiki effectiveness.
- The "near-zero maintenance cost" claim assumes ideal LLM performance; real-world error accumulation could increase costs.
