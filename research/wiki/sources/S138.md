# Cherry Servers: OpenClaw Hardware Requirements

> Deployment guide | 2026-04-22

- Minimum RAM: 4 GB (test/lab), 8 GB recommended for smoother experience
- Minimum CPU: dual-core or quad-core (Intel i3, AMD Ryzen 3)
