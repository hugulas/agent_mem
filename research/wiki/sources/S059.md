# 36kr: 库克谈Apple Intelligence

> Chinese industry analysis | 2026-02

## 1. Core Claim
- Apple Intelligence currently functions as a "nice-to-have" rather than a "must-have" feature; Siri's comprehensive overhaul has been delayed to 2026, with advanced personal context understanding and on-screen action-taking postponed, revealing the gap between Apple's AI ambition and delivery timeline.

## 2. Method / Mechanism
- **Two-stage Siri redo**:
  - **Stage 1** (target iOS 26.4, Spring 2026): Deliver personal context + cross-app execution capabilities promised at WWDC 2024.
  - **Stage 2** (codename "Campos"): Transform Siri into a chatbot-like system-level entry point, deeper integration across iPhone/iPad/Mac.
- **Apple-Google deal**: Reuters reports a multi-year agreement to use Gemini models in Siri and other AI functions; Apple has not commented.
- **Future hardware**: AI glasses (screenless first, ~2027) and AI Pin explorations both depend on Siri competence.

## 3. Key Evidence
- Tim Cook acknowledged "AI-driven new products and services" in an internal meeting with unusual urgency.
- Advanced Siri capabilities (personal context, screen awareness, cross-app actions) confirmed delayed to 2026.
- iOS version naming shifted to year-based alignment (iOS 26), making delivery timelines more transparent.
- Bloomberg reports Apple accelerated smart glasses project while deprioritizing cheaper Vision Pro.

## 4. Limitations & Gaps
- Chinese tech media analysis (36kr/极客公园), not official Apple communications.
- Relies heavily on Bloomberg/Reuters reporting and unnamed sources.
- Some speculation about future products (AI Pin, glasses timeline) without direct evidence.
- Author admits Apple Intelligence is personally useful but not essential, which is subjective.
