## 1. Core Claim
- UI-TARS-1.5 is a state-of-the-art multimodal agent for GUI operation and game reasoning, achieving SOTA across seven benchmarks through a "think-then-act" mechanism that reduces errors by 38% and compute cost by 43% compared to GPT-4V.

## 2. Method / Mechanism
- **Native agent model**: End-to-end trained vision-language model with integrated perception-reasoning-memory-action structure.
- **Think-then-act**: Reinforcement learning-enhanced deliberation module enables the model to plan before executing, improving generalization.
- **Four technical dimensions**: Enhanced visual perception, System 2 reasoning, unified action modeling, self-evolving training paradigm.
- **Visual encoder**: 1120×1120 resolution with <5 pixel coordinate error.
- **Training data**: 1.5B GUI-specific training examples.

## 3. Key Evidence
- **ScreenSpotPro**: 61.6% (vs Claude 27.7%, CUA 23.4%, previous SOTA 43.6%).
- **Poki mini-games**: Good performance across 14 diverse games with up to 1000 interaction steps.
- **MineRL**: Highest success rate in "finding/destroying blocks" and "defeating hostile creatures" when thinking module enabled.
- **Error reduction**: "Think-before-act" reduces errors by 38%.
- **Compute efficiency**: 43% lower compute cost than GPT-4V.
- **Open source**: GitHub, website (seed-tars.com), arXiv paper (2501.12326).
