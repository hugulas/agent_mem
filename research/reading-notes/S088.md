## 1. Core Claim
- RetainDB offers transparent, tiered pricing from free to enterprise, positioning itself as accessible for prototyping through high-volume production workloads.

## 2. Method / Mechanism
- **Free tier**: 5,000 queries/month, 1 project, 1 API key, MCP server access, community support.
- **Pro ($20/mo)**: 100,000 queries/month, 5 projects, 10 API keys, 5 team seats, SDK + MCP + Memory Router, Knowledge Graph + Delta Compression, priority email support.
- **Scale ($99/mo)**: 500,000 queries/month, unlimited projects/API keys, 15 team seats.
- **Max ($299/mo)**: 2,000,000 queries/month, unlimited projects/API keys, 50 team seats, custom integrations.
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited queries, dedicated infra, SLA, SSO, security review.
- Annual billing offers a 20% discount.

## 3. Key Evidence
- Clear per-tier query limits and seat counts.
- Pro tier at $20/mo aligns with S089's pricing data.
- Delta Compression and Knowledge Graph are gated behind paid tiers (Pro and above).

## 4. Limitations & Gaps
- Free tier is minimal (5K queries/month) — insufficient for any non-trivial agent deployment.
- Pricing page presents cloud-only plans, which conflicts with S087's open-source/self-hostable positioning.
- Credit card required for free trial.
- No mention of data residency, HIPAA, or SOC2 compliance on pricing page.
