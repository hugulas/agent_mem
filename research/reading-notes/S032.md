## 1. Core Claim
Ollama's quantified local model deployment guidelines show a steep RAM/VRAM scaling curve: 8 GB VRAM / 16 GB unified RAM for 7B-8B models, 16 GB VRAM / 32 GB RAM for 12B-14B, 24 GB+ VRAM for 30B+, and 48 GB+ for 70B parameter models.

## 2. Method / Mechanism
- Model-size-tiered hardware requirements based on empirical Ollama deployment experience
- Distinction between discrete GPU VRAM and unified memory (Apple Silicon) requirements
- Guidelines factor in OS overhead and quantization trade-offs (implied by VRAM-to-parameter ratios)

## 3. Key Evidence
- 7B-8B models: 8 GB VRAM or 16 GB unified RAM
- 12B-14B models: 16 GB VRAM or 32 GB RAM
- 30B+ models: 24 GB+ VRAM
- 70B models: 48 GB+ VRAM

## 4. Limitations & Gaps

- No throughput or tokens/second data at each tier
- "Unified RAM" numbers may not account for macOS system overhead
